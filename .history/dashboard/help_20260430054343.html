Frequently Asked Questions

Deposits via Paystack reflect instantly once the payment is confirmed. If your balance hasn't updated after 5 minutes, please contact support with your payment reference.
Withdrawals are processed within 2–3 working days after submission. You will receive the funds directly to your provided account. Weekends and public holidays are not counted as working days.
You must verify both your email address and phone number before you can withdraw. Also, you must wait 2–3 working days after your last deposit before making a withdrawal.
Share your unique referral code or link with friends. When they sign up and make their first deposit, you automatically earn GHS 10.00 credited to your wallet. There is no limit to how many people you can refer.
Profit is calculated and credited automatically based on your investment plan. Weekly plans credit every 7 days, while fixed plans accrue daily and pay out at the end of their cycle.
Go to the Investments page. Standard plans require a one-time GHS 500 activation fee, and Premium plans require GHS 1,000. After activation, all plans in that tier are unlocked permanently.
