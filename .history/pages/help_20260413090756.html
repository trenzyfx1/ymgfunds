Popular Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Log in to your account and navigate to your wallet. Select the deposit option, enter the amount you wish to add, and choose your preferred payment method. Once the payment is confirmed, your wallet balance will be updated accordingly.

Withdrawal processing times depend on your selected plan and payment method. Flexible plan withdrawals are generally processed within a short period, while fixed deposit withdrawals are available after the plan's maturity date. You will be able to track your withdrawal status from your dashboard.

Returns are not guaranteed and may vary depending on market conditions and overall platform performance. We encourage all users to read the full terms of their selected plan before investing.

Start by identifying your financial goals. If you want flexible access to your funds, the Starter Savings plan is a good fit. If you can commit for a set period and want higher returns, the Fixed Deposit plan is worth considering. For compound growth over time, explore the Growth Plus plan. Visit our Plans page for a full side-by-side comparison.

Your dashboard gives you a complete overview of your account — including your total balance, profits earned, active plans, and full transaction history. All information is updated in real time so you always know exactly where your money stands.

If you experience any issue with your account, first check your transaction history and dashboard for any useful information. If the issue persists, reach out to our support team through the Contact Us page and describe your problem clearly. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
