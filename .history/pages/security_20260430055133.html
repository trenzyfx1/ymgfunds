Your Role in Security

How You Can Stay Safe

Security is a shared responsibility. Here are simple steps you can take to keep your account safe.

Keep Login Details Private

Never share your password or account credentials with anyone — including people claiming to be from YMG IQ support.

Use a Strong Password

Create a password that is unique, difficult to guess, and not used on any other platform. Change it regularly for extra protection.

Do Not Share Account Access

Your account is personal. Do not allow others to log in on your behalf or access your dashboard without your direct supervision.

Monitor Your Activity

Check your transaction history regularly. If you notice any activity you do not recognise, contact support immediately.
