Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how YMG Funds works.

Getting Started

Getting started is straightforward. Create a free account, deposit funds into your personal wallet using your preferred payment method, and then choose a savings or investment plan that fits your financial goals. From there, your money begins growing based on the plan you selected.

Some features may require basic identity verification (KYC) to ensure account security and prevent fraud. This is an optional but recommended step that adds an extra layer of protection to your account.

Yes. The platform is fully responsive and designed to work smoothly across all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop. You can manage your account from anywhere at any time.

Deposits & Withdrawals

Deposits are processed based on the selected payment method and are usually reflected in your wallet within a short period. Processing times may vary depending on the payment channel used.

Withdrawals depend on the type of plan you selected. Flexible plans allow you to withdraw at any time, while fixed deposit plans may require you to wait until the end of the agreed duration before accessing your funds. All withdrawal rules are clearly stated before you choose a plan.

Withdrawals can be requested directly from your dashboard. The process is straightforward — select the amount you wish to withdraw, confirm the request, and the funds will be processed according to your plan's withdrawal terms.

Growth & Returns

Funds grow at a fixed or structured rate depending on the plan you selected. Growth is calculated based on your deposit amount and plan duration, and is tracked and updated clearly in your dashboard so you always know where your money stands.

Returns are not guaranteed and may vary depending on market conditions and overall platform performance. We encourage all users to read and understand the terms of their selected plan before investing.

Growth begins after your deposit has been confirmed and your chosen plan is active. Funds need to remain in your account for a minimum period before interest begins to accrue, depending on the plan selected.

Security & Trust

YMG Funds is designed with secure account systems and transparent tracking to help protect user funds and data. Every deposit and withdrawal is logged and visible to you. While no platform can guarantee 100% safety, we are committed to maintaining a secure and accountable system.

Yes. All deposits, withdrawals, and growth updates are recorded and clearly visible in your personal dashboard. You have full visibility over every movement in your account at all times.
